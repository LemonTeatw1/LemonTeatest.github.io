Acapella:

Intro Text:

Building Bridges Through Music

In high school, I joined both the acapella club and the choir team, two groups with different dynamics. The choir had a larger membership, reflecting the relative unfamiliarity of acapella in my country. I recognized the potential of these groups to inspire one another and decided to act as a bridge, promoting the unique art of acapella. I took the initiative to introduce voice percussion, performing live to spark interest and collaborating with choir leaders to form a trio that combined our talents. We showcased our performance at a school assembly, drawing attention to the innovative style. Additionally, I organized lunchtime practice sessions open to anyone curious about acapella, creating an inclusive environment. Over two years, my efforts led to a 50% increase in acapella club membership, with choir members adopting acapella techniques and vice versa. This exchange deepened bonds across both clubs, fostered team spirit, and introduced innovative singing methods that enriched performances on both sides.

Baseball:

Intro Text:

Leadership Behind the Plate

As the catcher for my high school baseball team, I played a pivotal role both on and off the field. Catching demands not only technical precision but also strategic thinking and emotional resilience. I took on the responsibility of analyzing game situations, adjusting strategies, and supporting the pitchers to maintain their focus under pressure. This required building strong rapport with multiple teammates, which I achieved through consistent communication and personalized encouragement. Despite starting my baseball training later than most of my peers, I committed to extensive practice sessions, including after-school and lunch breaks, to master foundational skills. Over time, I grew into the role of a reliable "anchor" for my team—an emotional and tactical leader. My efforts contributed to achieving the best ranking in school history during the High School Baseball League, showcasing not only the team’s growth but also my adaptability and perseverance.
